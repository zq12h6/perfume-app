Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers to common questions about products and policies.

How long does a perfume last on the skin?

Longevity depends on concentration and skin chemistry. Our Eau de Parfums typically last 6–8 hours.

Do you offer samples?

Yes — sample vials are available for most fragrances. Contact wholesale@halwa.example for sample packs and pricing.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days for unopened bottles. Contact hello@halwa.example to request authorization.

Can I order wholesale?

Yes — for wholesale information please email wholesale@halwa.example.
